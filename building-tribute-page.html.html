The Blockchain Timeline

A history of a world-changing technology

Here's the history of the blockchain technology:

  • 1991 - Stuart Haber and Scott Stornetta Work on The First Blockchain. Their first work involved working on a cryptographically secured chain of blocks whereby no one could tamper with timestamps of documents.
  • 1992 - They upgraded their system to incorporate Merkle trees that enhanced efficiency thereby enabling the collection of more documents on a single block.
  • 2008 - A document is published online called ‘Bitcoin: a peer-to-peer electronic cash system’ on Oct 31. The document outlines how to build a digital currency that is secure and transparent without a bank or central body. It’s written by an unknown person called ‘Satoshi Nakamoto’.
  • 2009 - The first collection of transactions (the "Genesis" block) is created. At 18:15 on 3 January, Satoshi writes a hidden message into the genesis block (first batch of payments) is created or "mined" which validated a crucial concept of blockchain.
  • 2009 - Jan 12, The first Bitcoin(BTC) transaction takes place between Hal Finney and Satoshi Nakamoto. Blockchain works.
  • 2009 - Oct 31, "The Bitcoin Market" is established and allows people to exchange paper money for Bitcoin. The public begins to recognize it as a digital currency.
  • 2010 - May 22, The first Bitcoin purchase is 10,000 BTC for a pizza ($25 USD) take place.
  • 2010 - Nov 6, Participation in the Bitcoin marketplace increases the market cap to exceed $1M USD.
  • 2011 - Feb 9, $1BTC=$1USD. BTC continues to increase in value and reaches parity with USD.
  • 2011 - Jun 8, $1BTC=$31,91USD. BTC reaches a new record high.
  • 2012 - Feb 27, $1BTC=$1USD. Bitcoin Magazine launches. Enough people talking about Bitcoin to justify a dedicated media outlet.
  • 2013 - BTC market cap surpasses $1BUSD. 10 times growth in less than 3 years.
  • 2013 - Dec, Vitalik Buterin Releases Ethereum White Paper.
  • 2014 - July, Ethereum Blockchain Is Funded By Crowdsale (ICO). The system then went live on 30 July 2015, with 72 million coins "premined".
  • 2014 - Blockchain Technology Company R3 is formed and forms consortium of over 40 Legacy financial groups commited to exploring and implementing Blockchain Technology."
  • 2014 - NEO Project Is Launched as Antshares by Da Hongfei and Erik Zhang.
  • 2015 - Linux Foundation Unveils Hyperledger To Enhance Blockchain development.
  • 2016 - Gem launches Gem Health Network with Philips Blockchain Lab. Interest surges in healthcare to connect the industry using blockchain tech.
  • 2017 - EOS.IO is Unveiled by block.one as a new blockchain protocol for the deployment of decentralized applications.
  • 2018 - Blockchain Technology Continues To Evolve Depicted by increased number of cryptocurrencies as well as Companies leveraging the Technology To enhance Efficiency.
